About the CEO

Rajat graduated from the Delhi College of Engineering in mid 2000s and started his career in Telecom; first as a Software Engineer – building applications for Nokia and then with Evalueserve as a Senior Telecom Analyst. In 2010, he envisioned an e-Commerce portal by the name of 'Living Room Canvas' which was to become a marketplace for trading works of art and home decor online. By the mid of 2011, Rajat founded Get Set Resumes to serve a growing need for good document writers in India. By 2013, Get Set Resume had grown into a team of 10 with some big B2B clients and thousands of retail customers. In 2015, Rajat launched a mobile platform called Rocket Resume Builder which today has over 10k users across the world. Today Rajat is working on creating new technologies and services that can help solve the recruitment problem plaguing companies..
